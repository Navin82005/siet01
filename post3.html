Powerpuff Girls

Sri Shakthi Institute Of Engineering And Technology, Coimbatore, organized the “POWER PUFF GIRLS-2018” in view of International Women's day celebration. This celebration is to expose the power and equality of women in our society. We are conducting various events like Bullock cart riding, Bullet Riding, Jeep Riding, Boat Riding, Horse riding, water etc.

These events are conducted to bring out the hidden talents in every woman. In addition to these events, a seminar on women safety is also organized. Our college girls have participated in these events. On Friday the event started off with Jeep riding by girls on a dirty pitch and rough and tough areas which marked a powerful beginning to the celebration. There were about 18 girls from our college and 50 from out registrations .we have 3 jeeps .the three jeeps were Ford Pajero Sport, SUVs.

The POWER PUFF GIRLS seminar was held on “Women's safety" at our college auditorium. The guests of honor were Mrs. Sree Jaya N V, Lawyer, Solicitor, England & Wales Mrs. Nanammal (Yoga Patti) and her son Mr. Balakrishnan also participated, Mrs. Gokila, Lawyer, District High court Coimbatore, and Dr.Kamalaveni Professor, Department of Women's Studies, Bharathiar University, Coimbatore. Mrs. Sree Jaya, a leading advocate in Coimbatore and has been recognized by US and UK for her many accoladed work, gave a highly inspiring and brainstorming declamation on Women's safety. She underlined about the peak problems faced by women in day-to-day life and spectacular ways on how to overcome such violence. She concluded her speech by saying both men and women are equal. Mrs. Nanammal who is 98 years old yoga instructor or popularly known as "yoga Patti", amazed the audience with her sizzling yoga moves.She has won "NARISHAKTHIPURASHKAR" award and also was honored with "PADMASHREE" for her incredible work. The old lady was full of energy and it inspired all the young girls to be filled with zeal and become an owner of good health.Thereby “empowering the women, can empower the entire generation".

Dr.Kamalaveni, Professor, Department of Women's Studies, Bharathiar University, Coimbatore, a young women educator and scholar awardee by NFED encouraged everyone about gender equality. She presented about the international women's day theme "Time is now: Rural and Urban activists transform women's lives". On 3rd March 2018 by 6.00 AM the day started with bullet riding 20 girls of our institution participated in this event, The main theme of this ride is to bring the awareness on “SAVE WATER, SAVE FARMER AND SAVE NATIVE BREEDS” they rode the bullet around 300 KM and crossed various districts such as Changampalli, Uudumalaiet, Anamalai, Palladam etc, our college girls geared up the feminine strength with complete energy. Our students participated Bullock cart riding, horse riding events this emphasized the never lagging attitude of women.

A Seminar on “Women's safety in the workplace” was held at the college auditorium. The distinguished guests of honor were Mrs. Divya Vasudevan, Founder, Agro Green Bio life, and Mrs. Prasanna Raghini HR Pavaaai. The seminar began by Mrs. Prasanna Ragin's inspiring and motivating words. She underlined the importance of career development for young girls. Furthermore, she added about financial independence and urged every girl to adopt a confident lifestyle. Mrs.Dhivya Vasudevan who embraced the audience with her cheerful and lively speech. She shared her beautiful life experience of hardships and struggles she came through. Yet, advised to take life positively and grab every opportunity available. Being cheerful and joyful at any situation was another thing she iterated. She amiably interfaced everyone and convinced the young girls to have a livelihood for their own and thereby leading an independent life. She encouraged girls to take up and face challenges. Prizes were distributed to the participants in various events.
